Rules of the Quiz

  • The quiz contains a series of multiple-choice questions, with a time limit of 10 seconds for each question.
  • You will receive one point for each correct answer. You will see your score at the end of the quiz.
  • If you want to retake the quiz, click on the restart button at the end of the quiz.
  • Each question has a time limit of 10 seconds. If you do not answer the question within the time limit, the quiz will move to the next question automatically.
  • Once you have answered the current question, you can click on the 'NEXT' button to move on to the next question.
  • The quiz questions are generated randomly from a pool of questions. The order of the questions and the options for each question are randomized.
  • After you select an option for a question, the app will check if the option is correct or not. If the option is correct, the app will display a green highlight around the option. If the option is incorrect, the app will display a red highlight around the option, and also show the correct answer.
  • You may click 'START QUIZ' to begin.
